About Me

Hello! I'm Bolametiren Akinlaja, a results-driven Program Manager at Bowie State University's Center for Data Analytics (CDA). I design and lead initiatives blending data literacy, workforce readiness, student engagement, and entrepreneurship. My passion lies in bridging academia and industry to empower students with the tools and networks needed for success in today's digital economy.

My Mission: To create transformative educational experiences that equip the next generation with the data analytics skills, professional networks, and entrepreneurial mindset needed to thrive in an increasingly digital world.

I specialize in project management, data analytics training, and strategic partnerships that prepare the next generation of leaders. With extensive experience in workforce development and a proven track record of securing funding for innovative programs, I bring a unique blend of technical expertise and strategic vision to every initiative.

My work encompasses coordinating high-impact events, managing complex training programs, and fostering partnerships with industry leaders to ensure students gain real-world experience and opportunities.
