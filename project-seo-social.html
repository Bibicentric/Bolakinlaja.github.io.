Project Overview

Directed a comprehensive SEO and social media outreach campaign to position Akiavic Blue Roof Hotel as a premier destination in Ondo State, Nigeria. This multi-channel digital marketing initiative combined search engine optimization, content marketing, and strategic social media engagement to drive brand awareness, increase online visibility, and boost direct bookings.

The campaign leveraged data-driven insights to target the right audiences, optimize content for search engines, and create engaging social media narratives that resonated with potential guests. Through consistent brand messaging and strategic platform selection, the project achieved significant growth in online presence and customer engagement.

Digital Marketing Strategies

SEO Optimization

Implemented on-page and off-page SEO techniques, including keyword research, meta optimization, and quality backlink building to improve search rankings.

Content Marketing

Created compelling blog posts, property descriptions, and local guides optimized for both search engines and user engagement.

Social Media Management

Developed and executed a consistent posting schedule across Instagram and Facebook with visually appealing content showcasing hotel amenities and local attractions.

Analytics & Reporting

Utilized Google Analytics and social media insights to track performance, identify trends, and continuously optimize campaign strategies.

Community Engagement

Built authentic relationships with followers through responsive communication, user-generated content features, and local community involvement.

Paid Advertising

Strategically deployed targeted ads on social platforms to reach specific demographics and drive direct bookings during peak seasons.
