Data Analytics Training Program

A comprehensive workforce development initiative empowering students and professionals with in-demand data analytics skills through hands-on training and real-world applications.

2023-2024
200+ Participants
Bowie State University

Program Overview

Spearheaded and managed a multi-session data analytics training program at Bowie State University, designed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry-ready data skills. This comprehensive initiative provided hands-on training in the most sought-after business intelligence and data visualization tools, preparing participants for careers in the rapidly growing data analytics field.

The program was carefully designed to accommodate learners at various skill levels, from beginners with no prior experience to intermediate users looking to enhance their capabilities. Through a combination of theoretical knowledge, practical exercises, and real-world case studies, participants gained the confidence and competence to leverage data for informed decision-making.
