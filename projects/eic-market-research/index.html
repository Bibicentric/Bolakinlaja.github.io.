EIC Market Research Collaboration

Empowering student entrepreneurs with data-driven market insights

Overview

Designed and delivered a comprehensive business consultation series in partnership with the Entrepreneurship & Innovation Center (EIC), helping 8 student entrepreneurs leverage data analytics for strategic business decisions. This collaboration provided professional-grade market research typically costing thousands of dollars, saving participants over $12,000 in consulting fees while strengthening their business foundations. As Research Consultant and Program Designer, I conducted in-depth market analyses, competitive landscapes, and customer segmentation studies that directly informed business strategy.

My Role

  • Market research consultant and strategic advisor for student entrepreneurs
  • Data analytics specialist conducting industry and competitive analyses
  • Workshop designer and facilitator for entrepreneurship education
  • Partnership coordinator between CDA and Entrepreneurship & Innovation Center
  • Report author delivering actionable business insights

Objectives

  • Provide student entrepreneurs with professional market research and competitive intelligence
  • Demonstrate practical application of data analytics in business decision-making
  • Reduce barriers to accessing expensive consulting services for student startups
  • Build sustainable partnership model between CDA and EIC
  • Support business growth through data-informed strategy development

Process / What I Did

  • Conducted intake consultations with 8 student entrepreneurs to understand business needs
  • Performed comprehensive industry research and market sizing analyses
  • Analyzed competitive landscapes identifying key players and market positioning opportunities
  • Developed customer personas and segmentation strategies using demographic and behavioral data
  • Created financial projections and revenue models based on market research findings
  • Designed custom data visualizations and executive summaries for each business
  • Facilitated strategy sessions helping entrepreneurs interpret and apply research findings
  • Delivered 8 comprehensive market research reports with actionable recommendations

Reflection

This collaboration reinforced my belief that data analytics should be accessible to all entrepreneurs, not just those with large budgets. The most challenging aspect was tailoring sophisticated research methodologies to early-stage ventures with limited data availability. I learned to be creative in finding proxy data sources and qualitative insights. The success of this program has led to it becoming an annual partnership, and several participating entrepreneurs have returned to share how the research informed critical business decisions. Future iterations will include group workshops alongside individual consultations to build peer learning among student entrepreneurs.
