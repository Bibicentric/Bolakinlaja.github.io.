Overview

Delivered 8 professional market research reports empowering student entrepreneurs, generating $12K+ in consulting value and strategic insights. This collaboration between the Center for Data Analytics and the Entrepreneurship Innovation Center provided real-world consulting experience for students while supporting campus entrepreneurship ecosystem.

My Role

  • Designed and managed market research collaboration program
  • Recruited and trained 10 student researchers in professional research methods
  • Matched student teams with entrepreneur clients based on industry and needs
  • Facilitated client intake meetings and project scoping
  • Provided quality assurance and mentorship throughout research process
  • Delivered final reports and presentations to entrepreneur clients

Objectives

  • Provide real-world consulting experience for data science students
  • Support campus entrepreneurship ecosystem with professional market research
  • Generate consulting value for student entrepreneur ventures
  • Build sustainable model for student-led consulting services
  • Demonstrate impact of data-driven decision making in entrepreneurship

Approach / What I Did

  • Established partnership between CDA and Entrepreneurship Innovation Center
  • Recruited 10 student researchers across data science and business programs
  • Developed research methodology training and quality standards
  • Conducted intake meetings with 8 entrepreneur clients to scope projects
  • Assigned student teams based on industry expertise and research needs
  • Provided weekly check-ins, mentorship, and quality review
  • Facilitated final presentations delivering insights and recommendations
  • Calculated consulting value based on professional market research rates

Deliverables

  • 8 Professional Market Research Reports: Comprehensive analysis and recommendations for entrepreneur clients
  • Research Methodology Training Program: Training materials and standards for student researchers
  • Client Intake Process: Structured framework for scoping and matching research projects
  • Quality Assurance Framework: Review standards ensuring professional-grade deliverables
  • Final Presentation Decks: Client-facing presentations of research findings and recommendations
  • Program Impact Report: Documentation of consulting value and student learning outcomes

Outcomes

  • 8 professional market research reports delivered to entrepreneurs
  • $12K+ in consulting value generated based on market rates
  • 10 students gained real-world consulting and research experience
  • 100% client satisfaction with research quality and insights
  • 3 entrepreneurs secured funding based on research insights
  • Sustainable model established for ongoing collaboration
