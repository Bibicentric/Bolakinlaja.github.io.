Smart DARTT Lab Modernization

Transforming BSU's data analytics infrastructure into an AI-ready innovation hub

Overview

Led the comprehensive modernization of Bowie State University's Data Analytics Research, Trading & Technology (DARTT) Lab through a strategic partnership with Truist Bank. This $350K+ initiative transformed the lab into a state-of-the-art, AI-ready data innovation hub supporting 80+ learners. As Program Manager, I coordinated all aspects of the modernization including technology procurement, vendor management, curriculum integration, and stakeholder communications to ensure the lab serves as a model for HBCU data analytics education.

My Role

  • Program Manager overseeing all aspects of lab modernization initiative
  • Partnership liaison between Bowie State University and Truist Bank
  • Technology needs assessment and procurement coordinator
  • Curriculum integration specialist aligning lab capabilities with learning outcomes
  • Stakeholder communications lead for faculty, students, and corporate partners
  • Project timeline and budget management

Objectives

  • Modernize lab infrastructure to support AI and advanced analytics education
  • Secure significant corporate funding to support lab transformation
  • Integrate cloud-based platforms and industry-standard analytics tools
  • Create experiential learning environment supporting 80+ students annually
  • Establish BSU as a leader in HBCU data analytics education
  • Build sustainable partnership model for ongoing lab support and innovation

Process / What I Did

  • Conducted comprehensive needs assessment with faculty and students to identify technology gaps
  • Developed partnership proposal and secured $350K+ funding commitment from Truist Bank
  • Created detailed technology roadmap including hardware, software, and cloud infrastructure requirements
  • Coordinated vendor evaluations and procurement processes for analytics platforms
  • Designed physical lab layout to optimize collaborative learning and industry-standard workflows
  • Integrated Microsoft Azure, Power BI, Tableau, Python, and SQL environments
  • Developed training materials and onboarding programs for faculty and students
  • Established lab usage policies and best practices for ongoing operations
  • Created metrics dashboard to track lab utilization and student outcomes

Reflection

The DARTT Lab modernization exemplifies how strategic corporate partnerships can transform educational infrastructure. Managing this complex project taught me the importance of balancing technical requirements with pedagogical goals while maintaining clear communication across diverse stakeholders. The most challenging aspect was coordinating timelines between corporate procurement processes and academic calendars. Moving forward, the success of this initiative has created a blueprint for similar lab modernization efforts and strengthened our ability to attract additional corporate partnerships for student-facing programs.
