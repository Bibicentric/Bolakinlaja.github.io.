Overview

Student-owned leadership engine fostering applied data skills, professional development, and talent pipeline for 250+ participants through peer-led programming. Data Tribe serves as the premier student organization for data science, creating a vibrant community of learners and future professionals.

My Role

  • Founded and architected Data Tribe as student-led data community
  • Recruited and mentored 8-person executive leadership team
  • Facilitated 50+ workshops, panels, and networking events
  • Created curriculum for peer-led skill-building sessions
  • Established partnerships with industry sponsors and speakers
  • Built sustainable organizational structure for long-term growth

Objectives

  • Create student-owned community fostering data science excellence
  • Provide hands-on learning through peer-led workshops and projects
  • Build professional development pipeline connecting students to opportunities
  • Establish sustainable leadership model for continued growth
  • Position Data Tribe as premier HBCU data science organization

Approach / What I Did

  • Launched organization with founding team of passionate data science students
  • Recruited diverse executive team representing multiple academic disciplines
  • Developed semester programming including workshops, hackathons, and guest speakers
  • Created mentorship program connecting underclassmen with upperclass leaders
  • Established industry partnerships for sponsorships, speakers, and internship opportunities
  • Built social media presence and branding to attract new members
  • Implemented feedback loops to continuously improve programming and engagement

Deliverables

  • Organizational Structure: Leadership framework and governance model for sustainable growth
  • Event Programming Calendar: Comprehensive schedule of workshops, panels, and networking events
  • Workshop Curriculum: Peer-led educational content covering data science skills and tools
  • Industry Partnership Network: Connections with sponsors, speakers, and career opportunities
  • Member Database: System for tracking engagement and communicating with community
  • Social Media Presence: Brand identity and digital platforms for community building

Outcomes

  • 250+ participants engaged across 4 years
  • 50+ events hosted including workshops, panels, and networking
  • 8 executive leaders developed leadership and management skills
  • 10+ industry partnerships established
  • 90%+ member satisfaction rates
  • Foundation for sustained growth and impact
