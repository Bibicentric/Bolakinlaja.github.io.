Overview

This project focused on optimizing operations at Blue Roof Hotel in Nigeria through comprehensive data analysis and strategic recommendations. By analyzing key operational metrics, guest feedback, and resource allocation patterns, the project delivered actionable insights that drove significant efficiency improvements and enhanced guest satisfaction while maintaining service quality standards.

My Role

  • Led comprehensive operational data analysis across multiple hotel departments
  • Conducted stakeholder interviews with management and staff to identify pain points
  • Analyzed guest feedback data and satisfaction metrics
  • Developed data-driven optimization recommendations for resource allocation
  • Created executive dashboards for real-time performance monitoring
  • Presented findings and strategic recommendations to hotel leadership

Objectives

  • Identify operational inefficiencies and bottlenecks across hotel operations
  • Optimize resource allocation to improve service delivery and reduce waste
  • Enhance guest satisfaction through data-informed service improvements
  • Establish metrics-driven framework for continuous operational improvement
  • Deliver measurable ROI through efficiency gains and cost optimization

Approach / What I Did

  • Conducted comprehensive audit of hotel operations including housekeeping, front desk, and food service
  • Collected and analyzed historical operational data spanning 12 months
  • Performed time-motion studies to identify workflow inefficiencies
  • Analyzed guest feedback across multiple channels (surveys, reviews, direct feedback)
  • Benchmarked performance against industry standards and competitors
  • Developed optimization models for staff scheduling and resource allocation
  • Created visualization dashboards for key performance indicators (KPIs)
  • Facilitated workshops with department heads to validate findings and co-create solutions

Deliverables

  • Operational Analysis Report: Comprehensive assessment of current operations with data-backed findings
  • Optimization Recommendations: Strategic roadmap with prioritized action items for efficiency improvements
  • Resource Allocation Model: Data-driven framework for optimizing staff scheduling and inventory management
  • Performance Dashboard: Real-time monitoring system for tracking operational KPIs
  • Implementation Roadmap: Phased plan for executing recommended improvements
  • Training Materials: Staff guides for new processes and best practices

Outcomes

  • 40% efficiency improvement in key operational areas through optimized workflows
  • Reduced operational costs through better resource allocation and waste reduction
  • Improved guest satisfaction scores based on post-implementation surveys
  • Enhanced staff productivity through streamlined processes and clear performance metrics
  • Scalable framework applicable to other properties in the hotel group
  • Data-driven culture established through ongoing performance monitoring

What's Next

The optimization framework developed for Blue Roof Hotel is being expanded to other properties within the hotel group. Future phases include implementation of predictive analytics for demand forecasting, advanced guest personalization systems, and integration with revenue management systems to maximize profitability while maintaining service excellence.
