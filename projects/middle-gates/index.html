Middle Gates Ventures

Building a multi-service enterprise powered by operational analytics

Overview

Founded Middle Gates Ventures, a multi-service business spanning logistics coordination, event management, and analytics consulting. This entrepreneurial venture demonstrated my ability to identify market opportunities, build scalable service offerings, and leverage data analytics for operational excellence. As Founder and CEO, I developed business strategy, managed client relationships, and implemented data-driven processes that enabled efficient scaling across multiple service lines while maintaining high quality standards.

Reflection

Middle Gates Ventures provided invaluable real-world education in entrepreneurship that no classroom could replicate. Running this multi-service business taught me resilience, adaptability, and the importance of data-driven decision making at every level. The biggest challenge was managing cash flow and operational complexity across different service lines. I learned that successful entrepreneurship requires balancing vision with pragmatism, and that data analytics isn't just a service offering—it's essential infrastructure for any scalable business. This experience profoundly shaped my approach to program management by demonstrating how to build systems that work, measure what matters, and continuously optimize for impact.
