DigiBibi: AfroTech Innovation Experience

Showcasing HBCU excellence in data innovation on a national stage

Overview

Created DigiBibi, a digital storytelling and innovation brand representing Bowie State University's Center for Data Analytics (CDA) at AfroTech 2025. This initiative showcased the pipeline of data talent and cultural innovation emerging from HBCUs, positioning CDA as a leader in digital equity and AI education. As Program Manager, I conceptualized the brand identity, coordinated partnerships, and facilitated meaningful engagement with industry leaders across technology and innovation sectors.

My Role

  • Brand strategist and creative lead for DigiBibi initiative
  • Coordinated CDA's representation at AfroTech 2025 conference
  • Facilitated partnership discussions with T-Mobile, AWS, and HPE
  • Designed promotional materials and digital storytelling content
  • Led student ambassador team preparation and engagement strategy

Objectives

  • Increase visibility of Bowie State University's data analytics programs on a national stage
  • Establish strategic partnerships with leading technology companies
  • Showcase HBCU talent pipeline and innovation capacity to industry leaders
  • Position CDA as a thought leader in AI education and digital equity
  • Create networking opportunities for students and expand career pathways

Process / What I Did

  • Developed DigiBibi brand identity including logo, messaging, and visual storytelling elements
  • Researched and identified key industry partners aligned with CDA's mission
  • Created comprehensive conference engagement strategy and attendee outreach plan
  • Designed digital and print promotional materials showcasing CDA programs and student success
  • Prepared student ambassadors with talking points, networking skills, and technical demonstrations
  • Facilitated on-site engagement sessions and partnership discussions during AfroTech
  • Documented connections and outcomes for follow-up partnership development

Reflection

DigiBibi represents the power of creative branding in amplifying institutional impact. This project taught me the importance of authentic storytelling when showcasing HBCU excellence.
