Overview

The Smart DARTT Lab Management initiative represents a transformative investment in AI-ready infrastructure and workforce development at Bowie State University. Through a strategic $350K+ partnership with Truist Bank, the lab was modernized to support 80+ learners with cutting-edge technology, collaborative workspaces, and industry-aligned resources. This project positions the Center for Data Analytics as a premier hub for data innovation, research, and talent development in the HBCU ecosystem.

My Role

  • Led strategic planning and partnership development with Truist Bank
  • Managed $350K+ lab modernization budget and procurement process
  • Coordinated with IT, facilities, and academic leadership for seamless implementation
  • Designed lab layout optimized for collaborative learning and research
  • Oversaw equipment installation, testing, and quality assurance
  • Developed lab utilization strategy and scheduling system for 80+ learners

Objectives

  • Secure funding to modernize lab infrastructure and technology capabilities
  • Create AI-ready learning environment supporting advanced data science and research
  • Establish strategic industry partnership for sustained support and collaboration
  • Support 80+ learners with cutting-edge equipment and collaborative workspaces
  • Position Center as a premier hub for data innovation and workforce development

Approach / What I Did

  • Developed comprehensive proposal showcasing lab modernization vision and impact potential
  • Cultivated relationship with Truist Bank leadership to secure $350K+ funding commitment
  • Conducted needs assessment with faculty, students, and industry partners
  • Designed lab layout optimized for AI/ML workflows, collaborative projects, and flexible learning
  • Managed procurement of 25+ high-performance workstations, GPU servers, and collaborative technology
  • Coordinated installation and testing with vendors, IT, and facilities teams
  • Created lab management system including scheduling, access control, and usage tracking
  • Launched lab with opening event showcasing capabilities to students, faculty, and partners

Deliverables

  • Modernized Lab Infrastructure: 25+ high-performance workstations with GPU capabilities
  • AI-Ready Technology Stack: Software suite for machine learning, data visualization, and analytics
  • Collaborative Workspaces: Flexible seating and team collaboration areas
  • Lab Management System: Online scheduling, access control, and usage tracking
  • Partnership Agreement: Formal agreement with Truist Bank for ongoing support
  • Impact Report: Documentation of lab modernization and student outcomes

Outcomes

  • $350K+ funding secured through strategic partnership with Truist Bank
  • 80+ learners supported with access to cutting-edge technology and resources

  • 100% lab utilization during peak hours with high student satisfaction

  • Foundation for future funding through demonstrated impact and partnership success
