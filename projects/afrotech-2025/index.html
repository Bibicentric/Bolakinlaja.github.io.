Overview

AfroTech Conference 2025 represented a pivotal opportunity to showcase HBCU data excellence on a national stage. By strategically engaging 300+ industry professionals and establishing partnerships with Fortune 500 companies, this initiative positioned Bowie State University's Center for Data Analytics as a leader in data innovation and workforce development. The conference served as a platform for industry collaboration, student recruitment, and institutional visibility in the competitive technology landscape.

My Role

  • Led strategic planning and execution of the Center's presence at AfroTech 2025
  • Coordinated with university leadership to align messaging and showcase institutional excellence
  • Designed engaging booth experience highlighting student work, research, and program offerings
  • Facilitated networking sessions connecting students with industry professionals
  • Managed logistics including travel, materials, and on-site coordination
  • Captured data on engagement metrics, partnership leads, and recruitment opportunities

Objectives

  • Elevate visibility of HBCU data analytics programs in national technology community
  • Establish strategic partnerships with Fortune 500 companies for internships and funding
  • Engage 300+ industry professionals in meaningful conversations about HBCU talent
  • Showcase student work and research to attract recruitment and collaboration opportunities
  • Position Bowie State University as a leader in data innovation and AI-ready workforce development

Approach / What I Did

  • Developed comprehensive conference strategy aligned with institutional goals and industry trends
  • Designed interactive booth experience featuring student projects, research posters, and live demonstrations
  • Coordinated with student ambassadors to represent the Center and engage conference attendees
  • Scheduled targeted meetings with Fortune 500 companies including Google, Microsoft, and Amazon
  • Facilitated panel discussions and networking sessions highlighting HBCU data talent pipeline
  • Captured engagement data through digital surveys, business cards, and follow-up forms
  • Created post-conference report with partnership leads, recruitment opportunities, and strategic recommendations
  • Followed up with key contacts to convert connections into tangible partnerships and opportunities

Deliverables

  • Interactive Booth Experience: Engaging showcase of student work, research, and program offerings
  • Student Project Portfolio: Curated collection of student work demonstrating technical excellence
  • Partnership Leads Database: Comprehensive list of 100+ strategic contacts and opportunities
  • Engagement Metrics Report: Data on booth traffic, conversation quality, and partnership potential
  • Follow-Up Strategy: Structured plan for converting connections into partnerships and opportunities
  • Conference Impact Report: Comprehensive analysis of outcomes, lessons learned, and recommendations

Outcomes

  • 300+ industry professionals engaged in meaningful conversations about HBCU talent
  • 5+ Fortune 500 partnerships established with companies including Google, Microsoft, and Amazon
  • 100+ strategic connections made with potential for internships, funding, and collaboration
  • 50+ student recruitment leads for graduate programs and professional opportunities
  • Elevated institutional visibility positioning BSU as a leader in data innovation
  • 3 immediate partnership opportunities identified for follow-up and formalization
  • Foundation for future conference presence and industry engagement strategy
