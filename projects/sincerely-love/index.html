Overview

Founded research cohort employing 5 students exploring mental health and data, hosting summit with 100+ attendees for wellbeing conversations. This initiative combines data science with mental health advocacy to create spaces for vulnerable conversations and research-driven insights on student wellbeing.

My Role

  • Founded Sincerely, Love research initiative combining data and mental health
  • Recruited and employed 5 student researchers exploring wellbeing topics
  • Designed research projects examining student mental health through data lens
  • Organized community summit with 100+ attendees for wellbeing conversations
  • Created safe spaces for vulnerable conversations about mental health
  • Developed research outputs including visualizations and reports

Objectives

  • Explore intersection of data science and mental health advocacy
  • Employ students to conduct meaningful research on wellbeing topics
  • Host community conversations destigmatizing mental health discussions
  • Generate data-driven insights on student mental health needs
  • Create sustainable model for student-led wellness research

Approach / What I Did

  • Founded research cohort with focus on mental health and wellbeing
  • Recruited 5 diverse student researchers passionate about mental health advocacy
  • Designed 3 research projects exploring student wellbeing through data
  • Conducted surveys, interviews, and data analysis on mental health topics
  • Organized Sincerely, Love Summit bringing together 100+ students for conversations
  • Created visual storytelling and reports sharing research insights
  • Built partnerships with campus counseling and wellness resources
  • Established foundation for ongoing research and community engagement

Deliverables

  • 3 Research Projects on Student Wellbeing: Data-driven exploration of mental health topics affecting students
  • Community Summit Event: Sincerely, Love Summit bringing together 100+ students for wellness conversations
  • Research Visualizations and Reports: Visual storytelling and written reports sharing research findings
  • Survey Instruments: Validated survey tools for collecting mental health and wellbeing data
  • Student Employment Opportunities: Paid research positions for 5 students exploring mental health topics
  • Partnership Network: Connections with campus counseling, wellness, and support resources

Outcomes

  • 5 students employed as mental health and data researchers
  • 100+ attendees at Sincerely, Love Summit for wellbeing conversations
  • 3 research projects completed exploring student mental health
  • 200+ survey responses collected on wellbeing topics
  • Safe spaces created for vulnerable mental health conversations
  • Foundation established for ongoing wellness research and advocacy
