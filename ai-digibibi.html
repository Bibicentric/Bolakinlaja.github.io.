Overview

DigiBibi is an emerging AI assistant designed to support student engagement, academic success, and personalized learning experiences. The tool integrates conversational AI, guided learning, and student support into one accessible platform.

Purpose

DigiBibi was created to:

  • Help students navigate courses effectively
  • Improve data literacy across diverse student populations
  • Provide personalized explanations tailored to individual learning styles
  • Offer on-demand learning support available 24/7

Key Features

Natural Language Q&A

Students can ask questions in plain language and receive clear, contextual answers

Assignment Walkthroughs

Step-by-step guidance through complex assignments and projects

Study Plan Generation

AI-generated personalized study plans based on course requirements and deadlines

Writing & Research Support

Assistance with academic writing, citations, and research methodologies

AI-Powered Tutoring Recommendations

Smart suggestions for additional resources and tutoring opportunities

Audience

DigiBibi is designed for students across all disciplines, with a focus on:

  • Data analytics and computer science students seeking technical support
  • Students new to technology looking for accessible explanations
  • Remote and online learners needing flexible support options
  • Students balancing multiple courses and seeking study optimization

Impact

24/7 availability for student support

Personalized learning pathways for each student

Improved engagement and academic outcomes

  • CDA Integration - Direct integration with workshops, trainings, and program materials
  • Expanded AI Literacy Modules - Dedicated content to help students understand and use AI responsibly
  • Multi-Language Support - Expanding accessibility to international and multilingual students
