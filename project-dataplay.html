⚽ DataPlay Sports Analytics Program

Education & Sports Innovation

2024
Sports Innovation | Data Education

Overview

Developed and launched an innovative sports analytics program that combines data science education with athletic performance analysis. The DataPlay program creates a unique bridge between athletics and analytics, helping student-athletes understand and improve their performance through data-driven insights.

Created comprehensive curriculum and tools that make advanced analytics accessible to athletes at all levels, from high school students to collegiate competitors. The program focuses on practical applications of data visualization, statistical analysis, and performance metrics in sports contexts.

Program Components

Performance Analytics

Tools and training for analyzing athletic performance metrics, tracking progress, and identifying improvement areas.

Data Literacy Curriculum

Educational modules teaching data visualization, statistical concepts, and analytical thinking in sports contexts.

Athlete Empowerment

Individual coaching sessions helping athletes leverage data for performance optimization and career development.

Key Features

  • ✓ Custom-built analytics dashboards for tracking individual and team performance
  • ✓ Interactive workshops on sports statistics and data visualization
  • ✓ Integration with popular sports tracking platforms and wearables
  • ✓ Mentorship connecting student-athletes with data professionals in sports industry
  • ✓ Career pathway development for sports analytics and sports technology

Impact

Bridged athletics and analytics, empowering student-athletes with data literacy and performance insights

Key Outcomes

  • ✓ Empowered student-athletes to make data-informed decisions about training and performance
  • ✓ Developed transferable data literacy skills applicable beyond athletics
  • ✓ Created pathways to careers in sports analytics and sports technology
  • ✓ Established model for integrating data science into athletic programs
