Overview

As Program Manager for the Center for Data Analytics at Bowie State University, I lead and support multiple initiatives that introduce AI tools, literacy, and innovation to students across disciplines. These programs create pathways for students to become AI-ready professionals while fostering a culture of innovation on campus.

Key Initiatives

Get Comfortable with AI (CDA Chat Series)

An interactive discussion series designed to demystify AI and build comfort with emerging technologies. Students engage in conversations about AI applications, ethical considerations, and career opportunities in the AI landscape.

AI Literacy Workshops

Hands-on workshops introducing students who are new to AI. Topics include:

  • Fundamentals of artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Practical applications across industries
  • Using AI tools for research and coursework
  • Ethical AI usage and digital citizenship

Responsible AI Learning Partnership with AWS

Strategic partnership bringing AWS's responsible AI curriculum to Bowie State students. This initiative provides:

  • Access to industry-leading AI ethics frameworks
  • Real-world case studies on responsible AI deployment
  • Cloud computing resources for AI experimentation
  • Certification opportunities in AWS AI services

AI Tools for Data Storytelling & Research

Integration of AI-powered tools to enhance student research capabilities:

  • Natural language processing for text analysis
  • AI-assisted data visualization and insight generation
  • Automated research summarization tools
  • Machine learning platforms for predictive analytics

Faculty Support for AI-Enabled Teaching Modules

Empowering faculty to integrate AI into their curriculum through:

  • Professional development workshops on AI pedagogy
  • AI teaching assistants and grading tools
  • Curriculum design support for AI integration
  • Collaborative learning communities for AI educators

Impact
