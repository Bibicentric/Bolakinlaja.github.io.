Overview

Founded and managed the Sincerely, Love Summer Research Cohort, employing 5 student researchers to explore love, mental health, and emotional intelligence through data. This innovative program combined rigorous research methodology with personal development and community impact.

Organized the Sincerely Love Mental Health Summit, uniting 100+ attendees for open discussions on well-being, research, and cultural connection, creating a safe space for vulnerable conversations about mental health in the Black community.

Research Cohort Components

Student Researchers

5 student researchers employed at $30/hr to conduct original research on emotional intelligence and mental health.

Research Focus

Explored themes of love, mental health, and emotional resilience through quantitative and qualitative methods.

Mental Health Summit

Community event bringing together 100+ attendees for discussions on well-being and cultural connection.
