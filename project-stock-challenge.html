Financial Education

Challenge Overview

Coordinated and managed the MSU/BSU Stock Market Challenge, a collaborative financial education initiative between Morgan State University and Bowie State University. This experiential learning program provided students with hands-on experience in stock market investing, portfolio management, and financial decision-making through a competitive yet educational environment.

The challenge utilized StockTrak's professional trading simulation platform, giving students $100,000 in virtual capital to build and manage diversified investment portfolios. Over the course of the semester, participants applied classroom concepts to real market conditions, developing crucial skills in financial analysis, risk management, and strategic investment planning.

Program Coordination & Responsibilities

  • Developed comprehensive challenge framework and rules in collaboration with faculty from both universities
  • Coordinated registration, onboarding, and platform training for 150+ student participants
  • Organized weekly workshops on investment strategies, market analysis, and financial planning
  • Facilitated communication between universities and managed inter-institutional collaboration
  • Created educational resources including trading guides, market research tools, and strategy templates
  • Monitored challenge progress, tracked participant engagement, and provided individual support
  • Arranged guest speakers from financial services industry to share professional insights
  • Coordinated awards ceremony recognizing top performers and highlighting learning achievements
  • Collected feedback and assessed program outcomes for continuous improvement

Student Outcomes & Impact

Participants in the Stock Market Challenge gained invaluable hands-on experience that complemented their academic coursework. Many students reported increased confidence in their financial decision-making abilities and a deeper understanding of how financial markets operate. The competitive element motivated participants to research thoroughly, think critically about investment strategies, and learn from both successes and setbacks in a risk-free environment.

Beyond technical skills, the challenge fostered important soft skills including time management, analytical thinking, and the ability to make decisions under pressure. The inter-university format also created networking opportunities and friendly competition that enriched the learning experience.
