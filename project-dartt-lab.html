Project Overview

Led the successful acquisition of a transformative grant from Truist Bank to establish the Data Analytics Research Trading and Technology (DARTT) Lab at Bowie State University's College of Business. This initiative represented a strategic investment in preparing students for careers in financial technology, data analytics, and algorithmic trading.

The DARTT Lab serves as a state-of-the-art facility where students engage in hands-on learning experiences with professional-grade trading platforms, financial data analytics tools, and real-time market simulations. This project exemplifies my ability to develop compelling proposals, build strategic partnerships, and secure significant funding for educational innovation.

Grant Acquisition Timeline

Phase 1: Needs Assessment & Planning

Conducted comprehensive analysis of student needs, industry requirements, and potential lab configurations. Engaged with faculty and industry partners to define lab objectives.

Phase 2: Proposal Development

Crafted detailed grant proposal highlighting educational impact, student outcomes, and alignment with Truist Bank's corporate social responsibility goals.

Phase 3: Partnership Building

Established strong relationship with Truist Bank stakeholders, presented vision for the lab, and negotiated funding terms and partnership agreements.

Phase 4: Grant Award & Implementation

Successfully secured $387,000+ funding, managed procurement process, and oversaw lab setup and technology integration.

Phase 5: Lab Launch & Sustainability

Coordinated official lab unveiling, developed operational procedures, and established framework for ongoing partnership and student engagement.

Lab Features & Capabilities

Bloomberg Terminals

Professional-grade financial data platforms providing real-time market data, analytics, and trading capabilities used by industry professionals worldwide.

Advanced Workstations

High-performance computers equipped with multiple monitors, enabling students to analyze complex datasets and run sophisticated financial models.

AI & Machine Learning Tools

Cutting-edge software for algorithmic trading, predictive analytics, and quantitative analysis using Python, R, and specialized financial platforms.

Financial Databases

Access to comprehensive historical and real-time financial datasets for research, backtesting trading strategies, and market analysis.

Trading Simulators

Realistic trading simulation platforms allowing students to practice trading strategies in a risk-free environment with real market conditions.

Collaborative Spaces

Designed for team-based projects, group research, and peer learning, fostering collaboration and knowledge sharing among students.
