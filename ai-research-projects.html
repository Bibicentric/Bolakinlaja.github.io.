Overview

These projects demonstrate the practical application of AI across diverse domains—from empowering small businesses to supporting mental health research and enhancing creative collaboration in the performing arts.

AI for Small Business Intelligence

Supporting the National Interest Waiver (NIW) initiative by helping small businesses leverage AI for competitive advantage. This project provides accessible AI tools and training to underserved business communities.

Key Capabilities:

  • Rapid Data Understanding - AI-powered analytics to quickly identify business trends and patterns
  • Decision-Making Tools - Accessible AI platforms for strategic business decisions
  • Efficiency Optimization - Automation solutions to streamline operations and reduce costs
  • Risk Reduction - Predictive analytics to identify and mitigate business risks

Impact:

Enabling small business owners to compete in data-driven markets without requiring extensive technical expertise or large consulting budgets.

Sincerely, Love – AI Emotional Insight Tools

Integrating artificial intelligence into mental health and emotional intelligence research through the Sincerely, Love research cohort.

AI Applications:

  • Text Analysis for Emotional Patterns - Natural language processing to identify emotional trends in written communication
  • Mental Health Awareness - AI tools to support mental health research and intervention strategies
  • Relationship-Focused Research - Analyzing communication patterns in interpersonal relationships
  • Sentiment Analysis - Understanding emotional trajectories and wellbeing indicators

Research Goals:

Combining data science with psychology to create new approaches for understanding and supporting mental health, particularly in young adults and college students.

Performing Arts x AI Collaboration

Exploring the intersection of AI and performing arts to enhance creative processes, cultural research, and storytelling.

AI Integration Areas:

  • Costume Research Support - AI-powered image recognition and historical pattern analysis for costume design
  • Enhanced Storytelling - Using AI to analyze narrative structures and audience engagement
  • Cultural and Creative Trends Analysis - Machine learning to identify emerging themes in performing arts
  • Digital Archiving - AI tools for preserving and cataloging performance art history

Innovation Potential:

This collaboration demonstrates how AI can amplify human creativity rather than replace it, opening new possibilities for artists and cultural researchers.

AI Bootcamp Programs

Intensive hands-on training programs introducing students to practical AI development and deployment.

Program Components:

  • Machine learning fundamentals and model training
  • Python programming for AI applications
  • Real-world AI project development
  • Industry mentorship and career pathways

Data Tribe AI Challenges

Competitive AI challenges designed to build practical skills and foster innovation among students.

Challenge Types:

  • Predictive modeling competitions
  • AI ethics case studies
  • Hackathons focused on social good
  • Industry-partnered problem-solving challenges

Cross-Cutting Themes

All of these projects share common goals:

  • Making AI accessible to non-technical audiences
  • Applying AI to solve real-world problems
  • Maintaining ethical and responsible AI practices
  • Building bridges between AI technology and human needs
  • Creating sustainable, community-centered AI solutions
